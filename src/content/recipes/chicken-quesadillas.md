1. Whisk together **2 Tbsp vegetable oil**, **2 crushed garlic cloves**, **1 tsp oregano**, **1 Tbsp cumin**, **1 tsp cayenne**, **1 tsp paprika**, **1 tsp white sugar**, **juice of 1 lime**, **1 Tbsp olive oil**, **1 tsp kosher salt**, and **1 tsp freshly ground pepper**.
2. Add **3 chicken breasts** and marinade to a bowl and massage to coat evenly.
3. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
4. Preheat a skillet over high heat.
5. Cook marinated chicken on both sides until nicely golden brown and cooked through.
6. Remove from skillet, let cool slightly, then slice.
7. Fill **flour tortillas** with sliced **chicken**, **Monterey Jack cheese**, and **sharp cheddar cheese**, then fold into quesadillas.
8. Heat a generous amount of vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat.
9. Cook each quesadilla about 1 1/2 minutes per side, until golden brown.
10. Serve with **guac**, **salsa**, and **sour cream**.
