1. Divide **1 1/2 lbs ground beef** into **8 equal portions (3 oz each)**. Cover and refrigerate until ready to cook.
2. Stir together **1/3 c mayo** and **1 tsp mustard**. Set aside.
3. Lightly butter the cut side of **brioche buns** and toast on a griddle or skillet over medium heat until golden brown. Transfer to a rack.
4. Increase to medium-high heat. Place 2–4 cold beef portions on the hot surface. Working quickly, cover each with parchment paper and smash into thin patties. Remove parchment, season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder, add a dab of sauce, and flip. Sear about 2 minutes on the first side and 1 minute on the second side.
5. **Add cheese:** Place cheese slices on half the patties, top with a second patty to make doubles, then remove from heat.
6. **Serve:** Place patties on toasted buns with prepared toppings and serve warm.
