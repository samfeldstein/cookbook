1. Heat **2 tablespoons** of olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add **1 small chopped onion** and a little **salt and pepper**.
3. Cook a bit, then add **1 diced bell pepper** and cook till the onion is soft.
4. Add **2 minced garlic cloves**, **chili powder**, and **cumin**. Stir for 30 seconds.
5. Add **1 can diced tomatoes**, **1 can kidney beans**, **1 can pinto beans**, **1 cup water**, **3 adobe chipotles**, **3 tablespoons adobo sauce**, **1 can corn**, **salt**, and **pepper**.
6. Simmer for 25 minutes.
7. Stir in **1 tablespoon lime juice**.

Suggested toppings:

- Sour cream or Greek yogurt
- Cilantro
- Cheddar cheese
- Avocado
