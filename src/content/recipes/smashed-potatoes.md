1. Boil **baby potatoes** with **salt** until tender (20-30 mins).
2. Drain and transfer to a baking sheet. Cool 10-15 minutes, then use a glass to gently press each potato to about 1/2" thickness.
3. Drizzle with **olive oil** over one side and sprinkle **garlic salt**, **black pepper**, and **fresh parsley**.
4. Bake at 450 for 12-15 minutes until bottoms are lightly golden.
5. Flip with a spatula, drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle again with seasonings.
6. Bake another 12-15 minutes until golden brown and crisp.
7. Sprinkle with **shredded Parmesan** and broil 2-3 minutes to melt the cheese.
