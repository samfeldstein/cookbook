1. Zest **4 lemons**.
2. Mix zest with **1 c sugar**.
3. Add **1 c water**, bring to a boil, strain.
4. Mix 1 part lemon juice with 1 part syrup.
5. Add water.

## Notes

You can make a big batch if serving a crowd, but if not it's easier to just keep the syrup in a jar and make as needed. And that way you can always juice a fresh lemon.

Bonus points if you make it in a shaker with ice.
