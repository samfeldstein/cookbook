<Recipe>

1. Cream **3/4 c butter** with **3/4 c sugar** and **3/4 c brown sugar** until light and fluffy (5-7 mins).
2. Beat in **2 eggs** and **1 tsp vanilla**.
3. In another bowl, mix **2 1/4 c flour**, **1/2 c instant hot cocoa mix**, **3 Tbsp baking cocoa**, **1 tsp salt**, **1 tsp baking soda**, and **1/2 tsp baking powder**.
4. Gradually beat the dry mixture into the creamed mixture.
5. Stir in **1 c marshmallow bits**.
6. Bake 10 mins.

</Recipe>
