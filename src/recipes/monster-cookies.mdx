<Recipe>
1. Combine **1 c brown sugar**, **1 c sugar**, and **3/4 c butter**.
2. Beat in **3 eggs**.
3. Add **1 1/2 c peanut butter**, **1 Tbsp light corn syrup**, **2 tsp baking soda**, and **2 tsp vanilla**.
4. Add **4 1/2 c quick-cooking rolled oats**, **chocolate chips**, and **M&Ms**.
5. Bake 11 minutes at 350.
</Recipe>
