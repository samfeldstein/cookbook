created: 2026-05-25
tags:
  - summer
---

1. Preheat oven to 450.
2. Shuck and de-silk the **corn**.
3. **Butter**, **salt**, **pepper**.
4. Wrap in **foil**.
5. Roast 25 mins.

## Notes

Last time I did this I made them ahead of time and put them in the fridge still wrapped. When I took them out they had a ton of flavor. Dunno if that's a thing or just a coincidence.
