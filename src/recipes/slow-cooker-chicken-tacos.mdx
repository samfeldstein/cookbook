<Recipe>
1. Add **1 lb chicken thighs**, **1 c rice**, **1 c salsa**, **1/2 c water**, **2 tsp cumin**, **2 tsp chili powder**, **1 tsp garlic powder**, **1 tsp coriander**, **1/2 tsp salt**, **1/4 tsp cayenne**, and **1 tsp black pepper** to a slow cooker.
2. Add enough water to cover the rice.
2. Cook on high 4-5 hrs.
3. Shred chicken.
4. Add **black beans**, whatever **veggies** you want, and a **handful of spinach**.[^1]
5. Cook another 30 mins.
</Recipe>

<Notes>
Suggested toppings:

- **Sour cream**
- **Cheese**
- **Avocado**
