title: Tequila Daiquiri
created: 2025-02-02
tags:
  - cocktails
---

1. To a shaker, add **two parts tequila**, **one part lime juice**, **one part simple syrup**, and **ice**.
2. Shake the shit out of it.
3. Serve up.

## Notes

Somebody ordered this while I was tending bar once in Marfa. He called it a tequila daiquiri, but as far as I know it doesn't have an official name.
