<Recipe>

1. Mix **3 Tbsp Earl Grey tea**, **2 3/4 c flour**, **1/2  tsp baking soda**, **1/2 tsp baking powder**, **1/2 tsp salt**, **1 c sugar**, **1 c butter**, **1 egg**, and **2 tsp vanilla**.
2. Mix **1/2 c sugar** with **1/2 Tbsp powdered lavender**.[^2]
3. Roll the dough into balls and roll them in the lavender/sugar mixture.
4. Place balls on baking sheet and flatten a little.
5. Bake at 350 10 minutes.[^1]
6. Mix **4 oz cream cheese**, **1/4 c butter**, **1 c powdered sugar**, **1/2 Tbsp powdered lavender**, **1 tsp vanilla bean paste**, and **purple food coloring**.
7. Frost 'em and eat 'em.
</Recipe>

[^1]: Don't actually remember how long we baked them. This is my best guess.
[^2]: If you can't find that at the store, just crush some lavender flowers.
