<Recipe>
1. Beat **2 large eggs**.
2. In a separate dish, combine **1 c bread crumbs**, **1/c grated Parmesan**, **2 tsp dried basil**, **1 tsp garlic powder**, **1/2 tsp salt**, and **1/4 tsp pepper**.
3. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and coat with cooking spray.
4. Cut **1 lb zucchini** into 1/2" thick slices and arrange on the baking sheet in a single layer.
5. Sprinkle lightly with salt, let sit a few minutes, then blot with a paper towel. Flip and repeat.
6. Add a handful of zucchini slices to the beaten eggs, turning them a few times to coat. Transfer, one by one, to the bread crumb mixture, allowing excess egg to drip back into the bowl.
7. Turn zucchini a couple times in the crumbs, gently pressing the crumbs so they stick.
8. Return the coated zucchini to the baking sheet, evenly spaced apart. Repeat with the remaining zucchini slices until they’re all coated.
9. Bake on the top-third rack at 425 for 10 mins. Flip the zucchini and bake until lightly golden, another 8 to 10 minutes.
</Recipe>

<Notes>
Goes well with **marinara**.
</Notes>
